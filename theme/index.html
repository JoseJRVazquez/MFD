ABOUT US

We are a family diner that serves American Comfort food 360 days a year. Nestled amongst the corn in the college town Macomb, Illinois, we are close to Western Illinois University.

When we say we are a family diner, we mean family (as in we all work here). It also means we are family friendly, so guests with babys and children are always welcome. It also means we don't sell alcohol, and that we close early on Sunday's to spend quality time with each other at home.

We hope you'll stop by and let us treat you like family soon!
